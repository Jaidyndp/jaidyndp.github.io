Drake Info.

Drake is a Canadian rapper and singer. He is accredited with his lyricism mixed with R&B beats. His career started as an actor on show called "Degrassi" in 2001. That same year his rap career took off.

Today:

  • Has heavily influenced the rap culture.
  • Has won 5 Grammys, and multiple other awards.
  • Is a household name internationally.
  • Continues to produce music for himself and other artist.

Bryson Tiller Info.

Bryson Tiller is an American rapper and singer. He focuses on R&B music. His carreer started in 2011 with a mixtape, however one song in particular lead to his rise. "Don't" released on SoundCloud drew many producers to him. Co-signs from Timbaland and Drake lead to major label attention.

Today:

  • Hasn't released music since 2020.
  • Supposedly releasing a new album in 2023.
  • Hasn't won a Grammy, but has won other awards.

Tory Lanez Info.

Tory Lanez is a Canadian rapper and singer. He started producing music videos for many artists, such as Justin Beiber and Sean Kingston at a young age. Lanez produced a lot of music prior to his career taking off. He rose up with an album named "Conflicts of my Soul. This led him to sign with Benny Blanco's record label through "Interscope Records".

Today:

  • Ghost-writes for many major artists.
  • Hasn't won a Grammy, but written for artists who have.
  • His future in music is unknown, as he is headed for jail following a violent attack on a female rapper.
